About us

Welcome to our Festival, an event dedicated to celebrating art, culture, and community! Our goal is to bring together people from all walks of life to experience and appreciate the creative diversity of our region. From music and dance to visual arts and gastronomy, our festival provides a platform for local and international artists to showcase their talents and engage with the audience.

Festival Timeline:

2011
The First Festival

In the summer of 2011, we held our inaugural festival, attracting over 500 attendees. The event featured local musicians, artists, and food vendors, showcasing the rich cultural tapestry of our community. The success of the first festival inspired us to make it an annual event.

2025
Looking Ahead

As we approach our next festival in 2025, we are excited to continue our mission of promoting art and culture. We are planning new initiatives to support local artists and enhance community engagement, ensuring that our festival remains a vibrant celebration for years to come.

2010
The Inception

he idea for our festival was born in 2010, when a group of passionate artists and community members came together to celebrate local culture and creativity. The first planning meetings were held, laying the groundwork for what would become an annual tradition.

 FAQ

Are there any age restrictions for attendees?

The festival is designed to be family-friendly and welcomes attendees of all ages. Some performances or workshops may have specific age recommendations, which will be clearly indicated in the event schedule.

How can I participate in the festival? 

There are several ways to participate! You can attend as a visitor, volunteer to help with event logistics, or apply to showcase your artistic work

When and where will the festival take place?

The festival will take place on 23 July at Central Park Chicago. We will provide detailed information about the schedule and specific venues on our website as the event approaches.

What safety measures will be in place during the festival?

he safety of our attendees is our top priority. We will implement health and safety protocols in accordance with local guidelines, including sanitation stations, social distancing measures, and capacity limits. Updates on safety measures will be provided closer to the event date.

Is there an admission fee?

Yes, there is an admission fee to attend the festival. Ticket prices vary depending on the type of access you choose . We also offer discounts for students and  seniors. Please check our "Tickets" page for more details.

Can I bring my own food and drinks to the festival?

Outside food and drinks are generally not permitted to ensure the safety and enjoyment of all attendees. However, we encourage you to explore the delicious offerings from our local vendors on-site.
